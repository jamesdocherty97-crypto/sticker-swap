# Sticker Swap Store Listing

## Short Description

Finish your football sticker album with the friends who have your missing cards.

## Long Description

Sticker Swap is a matchday companion for football sticker collectors who finish albums together.

Create a group, invite your friends, track the stickers in your book, mark your spares, and let Sticker Swap find the best trade inside the group. Your friends already have the stickers you need. Sticker Swap shows who can help, what you can offer back, and keeps every swap clear until the real stickers change hands.

Built for families, friends, school groups, teams, and offices, Sticker Swap keeps the ritual simple:

- Track your album and spares in a few taps.
- See group progress at a glance.
- Find useful swaps from the stickers your group already owns.
- Send in-app offers instead of messy group messages.
- Accept or decline offers clearly.
- Swap in person, then both people confirm the handover.
- Keep demo mode separate from real groups so you can try the app safely.

Sticker Swap is designed for the kitchen table, the classroom bag, the matchday sofa, and the group chat invite link. It is not a marketplace, betting app, live scores app, fantasy game, or payment tool. It is a friendly way to finish a sticker album together.

Unofficial fan tool. Not affiliated with or endorsed by any sticker publisher, football federation, club, league, or tournament.
