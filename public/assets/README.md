# Sticker Swap Public Asset Rules

The active visual system is "Matchday Foil Table": midnight pitch, cream sticker paper, foil shine, physical swap-table motion, and generic football-adjacent atmosphere.

Allowed:
- Generic sticker cards, paper edges, album slots, packs, foil sweeps, arrows, table surfaces, pitch lines, and floodlight atmosphere.
- App-rendered text in HTML/CSS.
- Fictional section codes and fictional sticker labels.

Not allowed:
- FIFA, UEFA, Panini, World Cup, club, national association, publisher, sponsor, kit, badge, flag, official ball, trophy, or tournament symbols.
- Real player names, faces, likenesses, official kits, or copied album/checklist layouts.
- Readable text baked into artwork.

Raster artwork should live under `public/assets/artwork/` with WebP or AVIF-ready filenames. Keep source prompts and legal notes in docs, not inside image pixels.
